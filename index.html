Hi there!

I'm a first year PhD student at the Institute for Natural Language Processing, University of Stuttgart.

I'm part of the research group Computational Models of Misunderstanding for Complex Instructional Texts (MisT) supervised by Dr. Michael Roth.

I'm working on natural language processing (NLP). My research goal is to model the potential for conflicting interpretations in written texts.

My research interests include

  • ambiguity, of the intentional and the unintentional kind
  • text structure, coherence and cohesion
  • the difference between implicit and explicit meaning
  • relation extraction and knowledge graphs
  • few-shot learning, knowledge distillation and other fun ways of thinking about resource efficiency in machine learning
